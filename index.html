Smart Kx offers fee accuracy assurance.

Smart Kx is the only client contract manager and billing solution designed for RIAs.

This revolutionary technology provides an integrated solution for Registered Investment Advisors that eliminates disruptive fee monitoring, limited billing approaches, and regulatory risk.

Fee calculating is a costly, inefficient, and daunting process. Current billing solutions may generate impressive reports, but they do not provide fee assurance, and your revenue is still subject to error and costly scrutiny.

The Smart Kx technology eliminates potential billing inconsistencies by providing a seamless fee calculation system based on the terms of the contract. With Smart Kx, the legal contract between the firm and client acts as a robust, dynamic, and autonomous calculator. Smart Kx ensures the contract and the fee are both exactly as agreed, thereby eliminating time-consuming manual calculation and review.

Revenue is no longer a report with Smart Kx. Your fees become part of a system that is programmable to any billing structure needed - simple or complex. The Smart Kx interface allows firms, clients, and examining regulators to view - in real-time - the contract, the fee calculated, and the custodial statement.

Thanks to Smart Kx, RIA revenues are protected, completely transparent, and proven on an immutable ledger.
